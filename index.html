Music Gate

Music Gate is a standalone mobile app built for music lovers who value privacy, freedom, and full control over their playlists. Unlike streaming platforms like Spotify, Music Gate doesn't rely on online services — all your music stays local, private, and offline.

No accounts. No tracking. We don’t ask for your name, email, or any unrelated data.

Full control over files. You can choose whether or not to grant storage access — or simply populate your playlists with your own downloadable files.

Minimalist and intuitive. Easily manage your music with simple tap and drag gestures — for example, just drag a song into a playlist to add it, or swipe it out to remove it.

Hidden features included. For the curious explorer, secret touches and tools await discovery to enhance your experience even further.

Our Philosophy

We believe that true digital freedom comes from decentralization. In a world increasingly controlled by large platforms and centralized systems, our mission is to build tools that return power to individuals, not corporations.

We create apps that work peer-to-peer, without relying on central servers. This isn’t just a technical choice, it’s a statement. Decentralized technologies help protect privacy, reduce censorship, and make the internet more resilient against outages or control by any single authority.

Our work is guided by a vision of a digital space where users are not products, but participants. We see a future where people can connect, share, and collaborate freely, with transparency, autonomy, and trust built into the foundation.

This matters now more than ever. As digital life becomes essential to daily life, we must build systems that respect rights, resist exploitation, and empower communities. That’s the future we believe in, and the one we’re working to create. 

Privacy Policy :

At Music Gate, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to user data and privacy.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of this app. All data generated by the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party.

Use of Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, share, or process any user data in any form.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect or process user data.

Our app does not collect personal information from children under the age of 13. As no data is collected at all, the app is compliant with applicable privacy regulations.
